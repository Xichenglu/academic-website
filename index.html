About Me

I received the B.Sc. degree in electrical engineering from Fuzhou University, China, in 2020, and the M.Sc. degree in electronic and electrical engineering from the University of Sheffield, U.K., in 2022. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science, Queen Mary University of London, U.K.
